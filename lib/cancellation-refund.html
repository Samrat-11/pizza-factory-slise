Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: August 2, 2025

Pizza Factory Slise is committed to providing the best quality food and customer service. This policy outlines the terms for cancellation and refunds for orders placed on our website.

1. Order Cancellation

As our pizzas are made fresh to order, we can only accept cancellations within a short window after the order has been placed.

  • An order can be cancelled within 10 minutes of it being placed.
  • To cancel your order, please call us immediately at +91 91827 14776 with your order details.
  • Once an order is sent to the kitchen for preparation (typically after 10 minutes), it cannot be cancelled.

2. Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed in the following situations:

  • Successful Cancellation: If you cancel your order within the 10-minute window, a full refund will be processed to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.
  • Incorrect Order / Quality Issues: If you receive a wrong order or are not satisfied with the quality of the food, please contact us within 30 minutes of delivery with your order details and a photo of the item. We will, at our discretion, offer a replacement, store credit, or a partial/full refund. The decision of the management will be final.
  • Failed Delivery: If we are unable to deliver your order because of an incorrect address provided by you or if you are unavailable to receive the order, no refund will be issued.

3. Refund Process

All refunds will be credited to your original mode of payment. Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.
