We deliver to select areas within Nellore, Andhra Pradesh. You can check if your location is serviceable by entering your pincode on our order page. We reserve the right to refuse delivery to any location outside our defined delivery zone.

Our estimated delivery time is typically 30-45 minutes from the time the order is confirmed.

Please note that delivery times are estimates and can vary due to factors like traffic, weather conditions, and order volume. We will do our best to deliver your order as quickly as possible.
